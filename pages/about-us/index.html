How We Are

Arias Labs System is a Spanish designing and engineering company that has its own development and manufacturing facilities specialized in production of laboratory and hospital furniture and equipment.

Our services include through our network of agencies and distributors in the world: Project designing, planning, engineering, and consulting. We provide you with the solutions required for your laboratory and hospital, having in mind offering you the best proposition in terms of price, quality, safety and delivery time.

Our Vevion

Our motto is "accessibility" regarding the quality/price ratio. We mean accessibility by offering our customers the top European medical and laboratory items.

Getting a safe laboratory and hospital with the required qualities should be accessible to any company or organization on this planet, in terms of price, quality, design and delivery terms.

That is what all our departments have in mind while working with our clients, designing and manufacturing new products for them. We want to keep it straightforward, safe, and transparent. When designing, we steer clear of extraneous expenses or extravagant designs that could raise the cost of production and the ultimate selling price without a good reason.
